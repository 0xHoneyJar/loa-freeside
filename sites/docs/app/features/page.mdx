# Features Overview

ARRAKIS provides a comprehensive suite of engagement intelligence tools for Web3 communities.

## Core Features

### [Conviction Scoring](/features/conviction-scoring)
Algorithmic scoring that goes beyond simple balance checks. Measures holding duration, trading patterns, accumulation behavior, and on-chain activity to identify your most committed community members.

### [9-Tier Progression](/features/tiers)
Granular member segmentation with up to 9 customizable tiers. Create meaningful progression paths that reward long-term commitment over short-term speculation.

### [Badge System](/features/badges)
Achievement badges for community milestones. Recognize holders who've been there since day one, survived market downturns, or accumulated during dips.

### [Shadow Mode](/features/shadow-mode)
Test ARRAKIS alongside your existing Collab.Land setup with zero risk. Compare results, tune thresholds, and go live when confident.

### [Analytics Dashboard](/features/analytics)
Understand your community composition at a glance. Track tier distribution, conviction trends, and holder behavior over time.
